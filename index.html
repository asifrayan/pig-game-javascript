How to play

  1. The game begins with "Player 1" as the active player.
  2. Tap the "ROLL DICE" button to begin rolling the dice. The number shown on the dice will be added to the current score of the active player.
  3. If the dice lands on "1," the current score is reset to 0 and the second player is given the opportunity to play.
  4. If you believe that rolling the dice again will result in a "1," tap the "Hold" button, and your current score will be added to the total score.
  5. The game is won by the first player to reach "100."
  6. To begin a new game, click on the "NEW GAME" button.
